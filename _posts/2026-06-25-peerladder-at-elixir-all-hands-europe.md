PeerLadder was showcased at ELIXIR All Hands Europe with a poster and three presentations – highlighted as the only country taking the ELEAD (ELIXIR female leadership programme) format forward and expanding it in new directions.

We were proud to present PeerLadder at **ELIXIR All Hands Europe**, sharing our work through a poster and across three presentations.

PeerLadder was highlighted as the **only country taking the ELEAD format forward** – and not only continuing it, but expanding it in new directions. It was fantastic recognition for the programme and for the community of underrepresented dRTPs it supports.

Huge thanks to everyone who stopped by the poster, joined the sessions, and shared their ideas for where the format could go next.
