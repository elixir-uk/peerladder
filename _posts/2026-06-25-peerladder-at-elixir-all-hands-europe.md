PeerLadder was showcased at ELIXIR All Hands Europe 2026 with a poster, two talks and a round table – highlighted as the only national spin-out of ELEAD, the ELIXIR leadership and mentoring programme for women.

We were proud to showcase PeerLadder at [**ELIXIR All Hands Europe 2026**](https://elixir-europe.org/events/elixir-all-hands-2026), across a poster, two talks and a round table.

## Building People, Strengthening ELIXIR

As part of the *Building People, Strengthening ELIXIR: Training, Leadership and Lasting Impact* session, Xènia Pérez Sitjà contributed to the conversation on PeerLadder's perspective on career progression programmes for digital Research Technical Professionals, while Saskia Lawson-Tovey presented a lightning talk on what the programme does and brings to UK dRTPs. 

[![Xènia Pérez Sitjà presenting PeerLadder at ELIXIR All Hands Europe 2026]({{ 'img/posts/all-hands-xenia-presenting.webp' | relative_url }})]({{ 'img/posts/all-hands-xenia-presenting.webp' | relative_url }})

## Laying the groundwork for an EDI focus group

We also led the conversation at one of the round tables of the workshop "*From Past Experience to Future Action: Laying the groundwork for an EDI focus group*", where PeerLadder became the inspiration for a new BeNeLux potential programme.

## Beyond ELEAD

In *Beyond ELEAD – implementing local activities that foster women's leadership*, we presented the case of PeerLadder as the **only national spin-out of ELEAD** – continuing the format and expanding it in new directions, beyond gender to all underrepresented dRTPs.

Huge thanks to everyone who stopped by the poster, joined the sessions, and shared their ideas for where the format could go next.
