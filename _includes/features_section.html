Made for You

A programme for underrepresented dRTPs

PeerLadder is for dRTPs from underrepresented groups – including but not exclusive of women, disabled and neurodivergent professionals, LGBTQIA+ practitioners, and people from minority ethnic or lower socioeconomic backgrounds – who are ready to step into leadership without fitting a mould that was never designed for you. RSEs, bioinformaticians, data stewards, data scientists, community managers and every other dRTP role welcome.

Genuine Peer Mentoring

Not a workshop tick-box.

A small cohort of peers who get your world – the culture, the challenges, the particular oddities of being a dRTP. Monthly peer-mentoring sessions and two workshops facilitated by OLS, specialists in open and inclusive research practice.

A Proper Programme

May 2026 – March 2027

Virtual onboarding in May, in-person workshop in Manchester in June (travel and accommodation covered), virtual leadership workshop in July, then monthly peer-mentoring sessions through March 2027. Roughly 1–2 hours per month outside workshops.

Are You Eligible?

Three things to check

Three years in a dRTP role, based at a UK research-performing organisation, and a genuine interest in developing as a leader. No prior leadership experience needed. Four short questions, ~150 words each — no CV, no references.
