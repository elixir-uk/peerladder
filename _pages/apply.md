                <div class="apply_section">
                    <h2 class="apply_h2">Are You Eligible?</h2>
                    <ul class="apply_checklist">
                        <li>You identify as part of an <strong>underrepresented group</strong> in dRTP leadership – including but not exclusive of women, disabled or neurodivergent professionals, LGBTQIA+ practitioners, and those from minority ethnic or lower socioeconomic backgrounds</li>
                        <li>At least <strong>three years' experience</strong> in a dRTP or equivalent technical research role</li>
                        <li>Based at a <strong>UK research-performing organisation</strong></li>
                        <li>Genuine interest in developing your leadership – formal or informal. No prior leadership experience required.</li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>PeerLadder is a positive-action programme: it exists specifically to support dRTPs from groups that remain underrepresented at senior levels. If you're unsure whether you fit, get in touch – we'd rather hear from you than have you self-disqualify.</p>
                </div>

                <div class="apply_section">
                    <h2 class="apply_h2">The Application</h2>
                    <p>Four short questions (~150 words each) covering your motivation for leadership, your capacity for peer engagement, structural barriers you've encountered, and your involvement in research communities. Plus basic career info. No personal statement, no CV, no references.</p>
                </div>

                        <li>Monthly virtual peer-mentoring sessions – Aug 2026 through Mar 2027</li>
